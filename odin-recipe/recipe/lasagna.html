Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide,flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese,between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Sausage
  • Red Wine
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Noodles
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layers
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
